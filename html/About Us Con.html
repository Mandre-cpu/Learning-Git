About Us

At Smart Build Construction Company Ltd., we uphold the highest standards of environmental, social, and ethical conduct. We are fully aligned with Nigerian regulations and international frameworks, including the IFC Performance Standards, Equator Principles IV, ISO 14001/45001, and UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights (UNGP). Our internal policies guide responsible behavior across all operations, from construction safety to anti-corruption. These include:

  • Environmental & Social Policy
  • Health & Safety Policy
  • Community Affairs, Health, Safety & Security (CASHES) Policy
  • Gender-Based Violence and Sexual Harassment (GBVSH) Policy
  • Human Resources Policy
