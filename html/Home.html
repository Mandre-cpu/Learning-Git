Breakdown of the Priority Projects for the N50 Billion Endowment Funds

S/N Priorities Budget Justifications
PRIORITY 1: Transcript and Innovation Hubs to Mark 50th Anniversary
1 Transcript Hub N1.5b To ensure ONE-STOP transcript systems that are constantly responsive to emerging trends and timely delivery.
2 Innovation Hub N4.0b To develop an iconic project as a mark of the 50th anniversary, making our products become industry leaders.
PRIORITY 2: Improvement in Learning Environment and Welfare
3 Solar Farm N5.0b To be self-sufficient in energy generation and contribute to the national grid. (The current recurrent bill on power is N2.832 billion).
4 Expansion of water supply N2.0b Upgrade the water supply system to meet the increasing population and demand for water on the campuses.
5 Upgrade teaching & learning infrastructure and expand internet N10.0b Upgrade, renovate, and equip learning facilities across faculties and expand internet infrastructure (fiber optics, NOC upgrades, e-boards).
6 Improvement in transport systems N3.0b Road works, bus stops with facilities, six buses, and electronic ticketing systems. Start layout for campus TRAMP (rail) system.
PRIORITY 3: Excellence in Research and National Development
7 Upgrade of Laboratories N16.0b Upgrade labs and facilities for each faculty to ensure global competitiveness.
8 STEM cell, biotech & nanotech complex N5.0b Specialized labs for advanced biomedical research (tissue regeneration, biotech in food & health). Will place UNILORIN at forefront of innovation.
9 Operational cost N3.5b Operational funding for sustainability of new and ongoing projects.
TOTAL N50 Billion
