About Us Weststar Associates Limited is the Authorized General Distributor for Mercedes-Benz in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The company’s brand portfolio includes Mercedes-Benz Passenger cars, vans, trucks, and buses. Also responsible for coordinating the manufacturer’s business activities within Nigeria, Weststar’s portfolio also includes dealer support, marketing, public affairs, training, overall strategic and operative sales developments, and after-sales services. A necessary strategic decision in preparation for the future in a growing market such as Nigeria. Its main dealerships and service centers are spread all over the country. To ensure seamless dexterity of its workforce, provision has been made for quality training of local staff which in turn, will be supplemented by foreign expertise from the manufacturers’ base.
